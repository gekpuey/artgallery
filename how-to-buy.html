How to Buy

Buying a painting from Testing Art Gallery.

Every piece we show is a unique original — no editions. Here's how to find one, ask about it, and take it home.

The process

Browse the collection

Filter by medium, subject, size, artist or price on our collection page, then open any piece to see its full description, dimensions and price.

Ask a question

Every artwork page has an "Enquire About This Piece" link. Tell us the artwork title and ID and we'll reply, usually within two working days, with condition notes, framing details and shipping options.

Hold or view in person

We can hold a piece for up to 7 days while you decide, or arrange a private viewing at the gallery. Many collectors prefer to see work in natural light before committing.

Payment & delivery

Once confirmed, we take payment by bank transfer or card. Work under 80cm is typically hand-delivered or couriered within 5–10 days; larger pieces are crated and shipped by a specialist fine-art carrier.

Good to know

Prices on this site include VAT where applicable but exclude shipping, which is quoted separately based on size and destination. All original work comes with a signed certificate of authenticity from the artist.

If you're new to buying original paintings, we're happy to talk through sizing for a specific wall, framing options, or how a piece is likely to age — just start from our Contact page.
